Who We Are

A Systems Integrator Built on Innovation & Trust

Taiga Systems Ug is a forward-thinking systems integration company empowering organisations through scalable, innovative, and customer-focused solutions.

Based in Kampala, Uganda, we bridge complex technology with practical business needs — offering custom software, cloud transformation, hardware sourcing, networking, and maintenance. Our work is guided by UN SDG 8 (Decent Work & Economic Growth) and SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure).
